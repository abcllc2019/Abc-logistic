Driver Hiring FAQ

Yes. Drivers must have their own reliable vehicle, valid driver’s license, insurance, and registration.

What shifts are available?

1st & 2nd Shift. You can choose part-time (3 days) or full-time (4 days) per week.

How much do I get paid?

1st Shift → $1.80 per package + $0.50 extra packages.
2nd Shift → $1.60 per package + $0.50 extra packages.

When do I get paid?

Pay is issued weekly (every Friday).

Where’s the warehouse?

Troy, MI 48083.

Is this W2 or 1099?

This is a 1099 independent contractor position.
